About

Jason Yeh is a photographer working across portraits, graduation sessions, studies, and movement-based imagery.

Based around Amherst College and Las Vegas, my work is shaped by clean composition, restrained color, natural light, and a preference for photographs that feel crisp without becoming overproduced.
